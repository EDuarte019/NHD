Immigration and Naturalization Act of 1965 has a purpose to reunite families, yet immigration continues to be a problem with no true solution.

Lyndon B Johnson

He signed the Immigration and Nationality Act of 1965, 30 years ago on October 3, 1965. Johnson assumed presidency after the death of John F Kennedy. So, he became the 36th president of the United States.

Immigration

An immigrant is someone who comes to live in a foreign country. Most commonly, people from mexico territory migrate to the United States. Mainly, to seek a better life.

Naturalization

According to the dictionary, naturalization is "the admittance of a foreigner to the citizenship of a country"

The Immigration and Naturalization Act

1965, the establishment of the immigration and naturalization act, many were based on their status Quo. The act was also known as the Hart-celler Act which consists of a system which promoted the unification of families.
